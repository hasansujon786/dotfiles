%A --> Day of week Expanded (Monday)
%b --> Month (Jan)
%d --> Day (31)
%Y --> Year (2017)
%D --> Month/Day/Year (12/31/2017)
%v --> Day-Month-Year (31-Dec-2017)

%T --> 24 Hour:Min:Sec (16:30:27)
%X --> Hour:Min:Sec (12:30:27)
%R --> 24 Hour:Min (16:30)
%H --> 24 Hour (16)
%l --> Hour (12)
%M --> Mins (30)
%S --> Seconds (09)
%p --> AM/PM (AM)
